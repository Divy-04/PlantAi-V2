About PlantAI

Built to help farmers and gardeners protect their crops using the power of artificial intelligence

Our Mission

Making plant disease diagnosis accessible to everyone

PlantAI was built out of a simple observation: farmers and home gardeners often notice something wrong with their plants, but don't know what it is or what to do about it. Expert agronomists are expensive and not always accessible.

We built PlantAI to bridge that gap — a free, fast, multilingual AI tool that can identify plant diseases from a single photo and explain treatment in plain, friendly language.

How It Works

From photo to treatment in seconds

1

Upload a Leaf Photo

Take a clear close-up of the affected leaf and upload it. Supports JPG, PNG, WEBP up to 10MB.

2

AI Analyzes It

Our CNN model trained on PlantVillage dataset with 39 disease classes predicts the disease instantly.

3

Get Your Diagnosis

See disease name, root cause, and cure. Chat with the AI for deeper guidance in your language.

4

Export Your Report

Receive the PDF report by email or download it locally. Includes your photo, diagnosis, and chat summary.
